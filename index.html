About

Duck Lau has been serving the music industry as a professional musician, composer, and producer since he won the Best Song Award at the 7th CASH Song Writers Quest in 1994.

Duck has worked with numerous major Chinese artists such as Jacky Cheung, Andy Lau, Leon Lai, Aaron Kwok, Sammi Cheng, Kelly Chen, Coco Lee, Eason Chan, Hacken Lee, Hins Cheung, Joey Yung, Miriam Yeung, Elva Hsiao, Andy Hui, Jeff Chang, Dave Wong, and Karen Mok... and many more.

Duck's repertoire is characterized by diversity, comprising not only Canto ballad but also R&B, techno, house, hip hop, trip hop, etc. As a composer, lyricist, and producer, Duck has written and produced hundreds of songs for the Chinese-speaking population.

Duck is also a professional singer and has performed in countless concerts as a backing singer, vocal director, and arranger, and has also recorded well over a thousand songs as a background vocalist.

Vocal Lessons

"I got a beautiful voice hidden inside. But I just don't know how to sing it out."

"I start feeling strained after singing for 30 mins. Voice is getting rough too…"

"How to make vibrato?"

"My falsetto fluctuates and is not consistent. What's going wrong?"

"I hope to sing strong high notes but I'm afraid of hurting my voice. What should I do?"

Duck Lau has years of singing and coaching experience which incorporates traditional and contemporary singing techniques. This contributes so much to successful and efficient vocal training methods for his students.

Singing skills vary a lot among different people. Duck Lau aims to provide adequate personal guidance throughout his coaching, which is significantly the key to progression.

Individuals and small groups interested in private lessons may direct their inquiries to enquiry@ducklau.com.
